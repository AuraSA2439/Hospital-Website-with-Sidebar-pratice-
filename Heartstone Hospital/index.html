About Us

At Heartstone Hospital, we are committed to delivering compassionate, patient-centered healthcare to our community. Founded on the principles of excellence, integrity, and innovation, Heartstone is more than just a medical facility—it's a place where patients and their families are treated with the utmost care, respect, and kindness.

Our dedicated team of highly skilled physicians, nurses, and support staff work collaboratively to provide a full spectrum of medical services, from emergency care and advanced surgeries to specialized treatments and preventive care. At the core of our mission is the belief that healing encompasses not just the body, but the mind and spirit as well. That's why we focus on creating a nurturing environment, equipped with state-of-the-art technology and modern facilities, to ensure our patients receive the best care possible.

As a trusted healthcare partner, Heartstone Hospital continually strives to exceed expectations through continuous improvement, research, and innovation. Whether you are here for a routine visit or complex treatment, we are dedicated to guiding you on your journey to health and wellness with the warmth and expertise you deserve.

Welcome to Heartstone Hospital, where your heart’s health is our greatest priority.

Facilities

At Heartstone Hospital, we provide a comprehensive range of medical services designed to meet the needs of our diverse community. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced technology and staffed by highly trained professionals, ensuring the highest quality of care across every department.

  1. Emergency and Urgent Care
    • 24/7 Emergency Room with rapid response teams
    • Urgent care for non-life-threatening conditions
    • Trauma care for accidents and critical injuries
  2. Cardiology and Heart Care
    • Diagnostic and interventional cardiology
    • Cardiac catheterization and angioplasty
    • Heart failure management and rehabilitation
    • Electrophysiology services for heart rhythm disorders
    • Comprehensive heart health checkups and preventive care
  3. Surgical Services
    • General surgery, including minimally invasive procedures
    • Orthopedic surgery, joint replacement, and sports injury repair
    • Cardiovascular and thoracic surgery
    • Bariatric surgery for weight loss
    • Robotic-assisted surgeries for precision care

...And many others...

At Heartstone Hospital, we strive to ensure that every patient receives personalized care tailored to their specific needs. Whether it’s routine health maintenance or complex medical treatment, we are dedicated to providing the best in healthcare for you and your family.

Contact

At Heartstone Hospital, we aim to make the appointment process as convenient and straightforward as possible. You can schedule an appointment in a variety of ways:

  1. Online Appointment Booking
    • Visit our website and navigate to the “Appointments” section.
    • Select the service or department you need (e.g., cardiology, pediatrics, surgery).
    • Choose your preferred doctor, date, and time from the available slots.
    • Fill in your personal information and confirm your appointment.
    • You will receive a confirmation email or SMS with your appointment details.
  2. Call Our Appointment Line
    • Call our dedicated appointment hotline at (123) 456-7890.
    • Our team is available Monday to Friday, 8 AM to 6 PM, to assist you with booking an appointment.
    • Let our staff know which department or doctor you wish to see, and they will help find a suitable time.
  3. Mobile App
    • Download the Heartstone Hospital mobile app from the App Store or Google Play.
    • Use the app to browse services, view doctor profiles, and schedule an appointment.
    • Receive reminders and updates directly on your phone.
  4. In-Person
    • Visit our hospital and stop by the reception or patient services desk.
    • Our friendly staff will assist you in booking an appointment at a time convenient for you.
  5. Referral Appointments
    • If you have a referral from another healthcare provider, we can assist in coordinating your appointment. Call our referral coordination team at (123) 456-7891 or bring your referral letter to the hospital.

What to Bring to Your Appointment:

  • Photo ID
  • Insurance information (if applicable)
  • Medical records or referral documents (if applicable)

At Heartstone Hospital, your health and convenience are our top priorities. If you need any assistance or have questions, feel free to reach out to us!
